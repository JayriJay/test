About Us


GOLDEN MYANMAR BOOK CENTER

GOLDEN MYANMAR BOOK CENTER is one of the most successful book shops in Myanmar that specifically gives modern books to customers. It was founded in 1990 as a small book store and gradually developed into biggest book shop in Myanmar. We deliver many book categories such as Modern Books, Children's Books, Magazines, Journals, Religion, Science, Management, Law, Bussiness/Economic, Comic, Education, History and Cook Books etc.
